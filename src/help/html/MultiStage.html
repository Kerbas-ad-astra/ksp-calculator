Multi Stage Calculator

A simple calculator that allows basic calculation of all a multi stage rocket needs. At the moment it does not take any burned out Solid Rocket Boosters into account.

You can add, delete and change stages and get the following results:

  • A list of stages with their individual informations
  • The total weight of the rocket
  • The total dry weight of the rocket
  • The total Δv of the rocket
  • The Thrust to Weight Ratio at the moment of lift off
